Discovery and Learning

The academic program at Barachel Christian Academy places an emphasis

on every aspect of student growth and development,

while meeting state requirements. Suitable class sizes and exceptional,

qualified teachers allow us to offer a comfortable

and inclusive educational environment, while ensuring each student

receives the individualized attention and support they need.

Grade 12 learners will graduate with an IEB school leaving certificate.




AEE ONLINE NSC (IEB) FAQS

1. What is an NSC?

• The National Senior Certificate (NSC) also known as a

“Matric” has been offered since 2008.

• Umalusi issues the NSC, however there are currently

three assessment bodies which facilitate

the completion of the NSC, namely the DBE, IEB and SACAI.

• Our students are registered with the IEB (Independent Examinations Board).

2.Why choose the IEB?

• The IEB has been accredited by Umalusi for the National Senior Certificate.

• It has been assessing the NSC since 2008 (prior to that called the JMB established in 1988).

• The IEB is at the forefront of innovations in terms of improvement of examination systems and processes.

• The IEB ensures that it keeps abreast with developments in assessment by actively participating in regional and international assessment associations.

• The qualifications assessed by the IEB enjoy international recognition.

• In 2018 approximately 12 000 full-time and 1 000 part-time candidates have been registered with the IEB to write at 250 examination venues.

• The IEB, through their assessments, are motivated to develop learners who are:

* critical users of information

* ethical reasoners

* problem solvers

* creative and reflective thinkers

* lifelong learners

* respectful of diversity, particularly in the Southern African context

3. Why choose the IEB online through AEE Online in partnership with Hatfield Christian Online School?

• Hatfield Christian Online School have been operating since 2009.

• They have been an IEB-registered examination centre since 2012.

• They have had 100% matric pass rate since 2012.

• And 100% bachelor degree passes in the last two years.

4. Why would a parent not remove their children from your AEE associated school?

• Parents have specifically chosen your AEE associated schools for many reasons including the spiritual input, nurturing and discipleship they trust will be provided.

• AEE Online is offering a significantly discounted rate to AEE associated schools, which the parents will not receive if they engage directly with Hatfield Christian Online School.

5. As an AEE Associated School, what is our role in the AEE Online NSC IEB program?

* To be responsible for AEE Online Academic Support

* To ensure that the AEE associated school supervisor provides

academic support to the students and performs his/her required duties and responsibilities.

* To be the first point of communication to the parents •
